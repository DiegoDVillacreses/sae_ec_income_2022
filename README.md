## Description
Results from an SAE-NER for mean income using the Ecuadorian Census 2022 and the Annual ENEMDU 2022. We propose Python code that uses the same methodology (but without the conformability condition) as Morales & Villacreses (2022).

Please recall that according to the UK Office for National Statistics guidelines (Chambers, Salvati, & Tzavidis, 2016), EBPs with a coefficient of variation (CV) below 20% are deemed publishable. According to the Ecuadorial National Institute of Statistics EBPs below 30% are deemed publishable (Morales & Villacreses, 2022).
